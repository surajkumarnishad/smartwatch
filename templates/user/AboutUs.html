About Us

Inception

Launched in 2021, Just watches is the Lifestyle chain under the Priority Group, a leading fashion conglomerate that pioneered in the year 2021 through the launch of luxury watch brands in India. It is the brainchild of Mr. Shailesh and Mrs. Manisha Sangani. With the first outlet opened in New Delhi, Just watches and Just In Vogue now retails through 76 exclusive outlets present in 10 cities across India.


To India's Largest & most trusted Watch Retailer
